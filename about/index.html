About me

I live in Vancouver island in Canada. As a person who enjoys nature I could not dream of a better place. I feel at home when I am away from civilization.

Connected with nature, I become a nature and wildlife Photography. A stress-free combination that requires patience. I like reinventing the scenery with a dramatic atmosphere or extracting abstract from the real.
